Playing phone games

Kimberly playing her phone games

Phone games can be quick and she can often play them during her breaks at work or as a quick mind break when completing her school work. Another good thing about phone games is that because they are available anywhere she can even use it as a time waster when she is out and about. By playing the online versions of cards games she not only has fun, but can also get in some practice for when she plays them with her family.

Playing cards

Kimberly playing cards

Another hobby of Kimberly's is playing cards. Kimberly has been playing cards ever since she was a little girl. Cards has become a very big part of family time for her. When her kids were younger she taught them to play cards too. There are many different card games that they play, but to name a few there is canasta, spades, three thirteen, and gin. Now that her mother is older she spends a lot of her free time at her mothers house to play card games with her. On holidays the family will get together and play cards. When people visit thy tend to play cards as well. Cards have really becoming a important source of bonding and quality time for Kimberly and her family.
